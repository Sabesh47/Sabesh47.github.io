Welcome to this comprehensive guide on incorporating Graymatter’S Redleads , a Leads Relationship Management (LRM) solution, into your website. In the ever-evolving landscape of online interactions, the integration of a robust and secure Leads handelling system is essential for businesses aiming to provide a seamless customer experience.

Setup Procedure


This step-by-step guide , we will navigate through the process of creating a Gray Matter account, generating API keys, and integrating them into your website. We offer a user-friendly platform with a suite of features designed to streamline the Leads management process. By following these outlined steps, you'll be equipped to enhance your website's capabilities, ensuring a secure and efficient experience for your customers.

Create a Gray Matter Account, Begin by visiting the official Gray Matter website and sign up for an account. Provide the required details and complete the registration process & Shortly our team will get in touch with you.

Access the Redleads Dashboard, Upon successful registration, log in to your Gray Matter account. You will be directed to the Redleads Dashboard, which serves as the central hub for managing your leads & prospect settings.

Navigate to API Keys Section, In the Redleads Dashboard, locate the 'Settings' option in the left-hand menu. Under 'Settings,' select 'API Keys.'

Generate API Keys, Once in the 'API Keys' section, you will find options to generate both the 'Key ID' and 'Key Secret.' Click on the 'Generate Key Secret' button to create a new set of API keys.

Securely Store API Keys, After generating the API keys, it is crucial to securely store them. The 'Key ID' and 'Key Secret' are essential for integrating Gray Matter with your website. Avoid sharing these credentials publicly.

Integration with Your Website, Now, integrate Redleads with your website by incorporating the API keys into your CRM software/ other software used for managing customers. This process may vary based on your website's technology stack.

Implement Redleads SDK, To enhance the user experience, consider implementing the Redleads SDK on your website. The SDK facilitates a seamless payment process and provides additional customization options.

Step 10: Monitor and Optimize, Regularly monitor your Redleads Dashboard for transaction insights and analytics. This will help you optimize the payment process and address any issues promptly.

By following these steps, you can seamlessly integrate Gray Matter’s Red Leads into your website, enabling secure and efficient online transactions. Remember to keep your API keys confidential, conduct thorough testing, and stay informed about updates from Razorpay for continuous improvement.
